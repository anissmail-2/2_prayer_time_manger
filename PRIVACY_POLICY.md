# Privacy Policy for TaskFlow Pro

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

TaskFlow Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**
- Email address (if you create an account)
- Display name
- Profile photo (optional)
- Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)

**Task and Productivity Data:**
- Tasks, to-do items, and their details (title, description, due dates, priorities)
- Spaces/projects and their organization
- Prayer time preferences and location settings
- Activity logs and completed tasks
- Custom tags and labels

**Voice Input (Android Only - Optional):**
- If you use voice input features, audio recordings are sent to Deepgram API for transcription
- Audio is NOT stored permanently; it's processed in real-time and immediately discarded
- You can disable voice input at any time

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:**
- Device type, model, and operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version and build number
- Language and timezone settings

**Location Information (Optional):**
- Approximate location (city/region) for accurate prayer time calculations
- Location is NEVER tracked continuously
- Location data is only used locally on your device
- You can deny location permission; prayer times will use default settings

**Usage Data:**
- App features used and interaction patterns
- Screen views and navigation paths
- Error logs and crash reports (if analytics enabled)
- Performance metrics (load times, response times)

**Prayer Times Data:**
- Prayer time calculation requests (via Aladhan API)
- Location data for prayer time accuracy (not stored on our servers)

### 3. Information from Third-Party Services

If you enable optional integrations:

**Google Sign-In:**
- Email address
- Display name
- Profile photo
- Google Account ID

**Firebase (If Enabled):**
- Cloud sync data (tasks, spaces, settings)
- Authentication tokens
- Analytics data (if analytics enabled)

**AI Features (Gemini AI):**
- Task descriptions and context sent for AI suggestions
- Natural language queries for task management
- Data is processed but not stored by Google

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses:
1. **Core Functionality:**
   - Store and manage your tasks, spaces, and schedules
   - Calculate accurate prayer times based on your location
   - Sync data across your devices (if cloud sync enabled)
   - Provide notifications for tasks and prayer times

2. **AI-Powered Features:**
   - Generate task suggestions and scheduling recommendations
   - Process natural language task input
   - Optimize your schedule based on prayer times

3. **App Improvement:**
   - Analyze app usage to improve features
   - Fix bugs and crashes
   - Optimize performance
   - Develop new features based on usage patterns

4. **Communication:**
   - Send important updates about the app
   - Respond to your support requests
   - Notify you of changes to this policy

### We DO NOT:
- ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
- ❌ Use your data for advertising purposes
- ❌ Track your location continuously
- ❌ Share your tasks or personal data with other users
- ❌ Access your data without your explicit consent

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage (Default Mode):
- All data is stored locally on your device using encrypted shared preferences
- Data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync
- You have complete control over your data

### Cloud Storage (Optional - Firebase):
- If you enable Firebase sync:
  - Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  - Data is stored in Google's secure Firebase infrastructure
  - You can delete your cloud data at any time
  - You can export your data before deletion

### Security Measures:
- Industry-standard encryption (AES-256)
- Secure HTTPS connections for all API calls
- Regular security audits
- No plain-text password storage
- Secure authentication tokens

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your data. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

### Service Providers:
**Aladhan API** (Prayer Times):
- Location data (city/coordinates) for prayer time calculation
- No personal information shared
- Public API, no data retention

**Deepgram** (Voice Input - Android Only - Optional):
- Audio recordings for voice-to-text transcription
- Audio is NOT stored; processed in real-time only
- You must explicitly enable this feature

**Google/Firebase** (Optional - If Enabled):
- Task and app data for cloud sync
- Analytics data (if enabled)
- Crash reports (if enabled)
- Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

**Gemini AI** (Optional - If AI Features Used):
- Task descriptions and context for AI suggestions
- No personally identifiable information sent
- Data is not stored by Google for training purposes
- Subject to Google's AI Services Privacy Policy

### Legal Requirements:
We may disclose information if required by law, such as:
- Compliance with legal process (subpoena, court order)
- Protection of our rights and property
- Investigation of fraud or security issues
- Protection of user safety

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control:
- ✅ **View** all your data within the app
- ✅ **Edit** or update any information
- ✅ **Export** all your data (JSON format)
- ✅ **Delete** individual items or all data
- ✅ **Disable** analytics and crash reporting
- ✅ **Revoke** location permission
- ✅ **Turn off** voice input features
- ✅ **Disable** cloud sync (use local-only mode)

### Account Deletion:
- You can delete your account at any time
- From Settings → Profile → Delete Account
- This will permanently delete:
  - All your tasks, spaces, and data
  - Cloud backups (if Firebase enabled)
  - Account information
- Backups cannot be recovered after deletion

### Data Portability:
- Export your data at any time
- From Settings → Profile → Export/Import Data
- Data is exported in JSON format
- Can be imported into another device or backup
